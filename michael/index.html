Sun in Pisces

The Sun provides us with our central vital source of energy. It represents the conscious self-hood of the individual psyche – the power to do and to be. It functions as the cloak our soul has chosen to creatively actualize its purpose, and the heart and core of beingness. This urge represented by the Sun is the desire for full self-expression, for manifesting personal power and talent in ways that inspire others to express their creatively. This is sometimes referred to as our yang energy, and it is traditionally where we shine.

The Sun rules Leo, and there is no other sign in which the solar light can shine quite as naturally. No heart glows harder or brighter than this. Leo energy is regal and dignified, proudly and grandly assuming dominion over the marvelous magical creativity of life. With a birthright to glory and unique personal access to inner child, the generous lion rules over the heart. Because of this, the Sun in Leo ferociously defends the innocent and the vulnerable – any sort of injustice or betrayal is a disgrace that has no place in its kingdom.

In the 7th House, the Leo Sun can feel that its light only truly shines when it shares with others the self-worth and substantiality of identity it came into the world with. It strives to radiate a basis for existence and to illuminate the path of selfhood for others – to reveal to others the own light within them by sharing its own heavenly light. The overwhelming need to be recognized and adored for its creative gifts and undeniable warmth can result in vicious self-criticism if others fail to provide it with the affection and reassurance it needs. Yet when provided with a deserved doting partner who sees the value of its compassion, the 7th House Leo Sun's inner glow is restored and recharged.
